Ethical Hacking Training

About The Course


Looking for ethical hacking training and you probably do not know where to start and you have no idea where to start. There is so much conflicting information out there on what to do and what to be experimenting with, what to read and so on. this is a prerequisite to all other hacking master training. Once you understand the concepts and fundamentals of this course we will be able to move comfortably to mastering hacking by starting from the scratch with zero experience , zero programing knowledge and no linux knowledge required. We teach you how to stay secure as well. This is a highly practical sessions but we do not also neglect the theory part as well.

Outcome of this course



We start by covering some of the required terminologies and show you how to set up the needed software by creating a penetration testing lab so you can practice legally and safely then we move to the main penetration testing sections. Moreover you will learn how networks work. How devices communicate with each other and how to exploit this method of communication by controlling all the connection around you with different means.
